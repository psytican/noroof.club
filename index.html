ENJOY & NAMASTE

OUR STORY

In the night silence of Limassol, on the border with the vast Mediterranean Sea, lurks a kind of underground paradise for alternative music lovers. NOROOF, as it is called, seems to call out into the dark depths of the night.

The club itself is a concrete space without a roof, 200 square meters of freedom and creativity. Far from the hustle and bustle of the city, in the Moni Beach neighborhood, NOROOF stands as a symbol of freedom and self-expression. The open sky becomes a vault under which life dances and pulsates.

The large club area was like a blank page where visual artists created their masterpieces. Light installations, graffiti and projections danced to the beat of the music, turning NOROOF into a magical place where sight is as important as sound.
